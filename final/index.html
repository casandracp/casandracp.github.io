Hot dogs are an all american meal but most likely it was german sausage makers who first came up with the idea. Tradictional hot dogs are made from a mixture of pork, beef and chicken. The cuts they start with are called trimmings, pieces left over from cutting steaks. Processed chicken trimmings are also added to the meat, as well as starch salt and other flavouring. Water is added to the mix and everything is blended together with corn syrup.
